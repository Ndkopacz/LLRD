Abdus is one of my favorite design partners ever. He has always been a strong research advocate, participating in research activities and advocating for team participation, but he also bases his designs on research findings.

For instance, during this Beta, he was using research findings to come up with design solutions as soon as he heard the feedback. Abdus's style of discussing designs is so positive. Abdus's ability (and willingness) to tackle so many huge projects simultaneously is critical to the smooth functioning of the AMI team. I personally get overwhelmed with my fairly light involvement across several of these initiatives, and Abdus somehow manages to drive all of them at the same time.
